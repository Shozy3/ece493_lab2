## Use Case UC-09: Make Final Decision

**Goal in Context**: An editor records an accept/reject decision once three reviews are complete so the author is notified.

**Scope**: Conference Management System

**Level**: User Goal

**Primary Actor**: Editor

**Secondary Actors**: Database, Email Service, Author

**Trigger**: Editor makes a decision for a paper with three completed review forms.

### Success End Condition
* Decision is saved in database, author is notified via email, and paper status is updated.

### Failed End Condition
* Decision is not saved, author is not notified, and paper remains in pending state.

### Preconditions
* All three review forms are ready/submitted.
* Paper has three completed review forms.

### Main Success Scenario
1. System has three completed review forms for a paper.
2. System allows editor to make final decision.
3. Editor records decision (accept/reject).
4. System saves decision in database.
5. System sends decision to author.

### Extensions
* None specified beyond the gating condition (must have all three reviews).

### Related Information
* **Priority**: High
* **Frequency**: Frequent during decision period
* **Open Issues**: None
